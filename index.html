About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Aerospace Engineering at Auburn University, specializing in robust trajectory optimization, obstacle avoidance, and Guidance, Navigation, and Control (GNC) for UAVs and spacecraft. My research focuses on developing real-time collision-free trajectory optimization algorithms for quadcopters and phase synchronization controllers for multi-rotor systems. I have a strong background in embedded systems, optimization frameworks, and computational tools like Python, MATLAB, and ROS.
