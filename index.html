About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Aerospace Engineering at Auburn University, specializing in robust trajectory optimization, obstacle avoidance, and Guidance, Navigation, and Control (GNC) for UAVs and spacecraft. My research focuses on developing real-time collision-free trajectory optimization algorithms for quadcopters and phase synchronization controllers for multi-rotor systems. I have a strong background in embedded systems, optimization frameworks, and computational tools like Python, MATLAB, and ROS.

Projects

Real-Time Collision-Free Trajectory Optimization

Developed an object detection and localization algorithm for quadcopters using YOLO ROS and ZED/Realsense cameras. Integrated neural networks for trajectory prediction and collision avoidance.

Technologies Used: Python, ROS, Gazebo, YOLO, ZED Camera.

Phase Synchronizing Controllers for Multi-Rotors

Developed and tested a precise speed and position PID controller for multi-rotors. Extended the controller to synchronize co-rotating propellers and control blade crossover in counter-rotating propellers.

Technologies Used: MATLAB, Simulink, Arduino, PID Control.

Global Trajectory Optimization Competition

Developed low-level and high-level optimizers for asteroid mining missions. Placed 20th out of 102 teams in the competition.

Technologies Used: MATLAB, Optimization Algorithms, Trajectory Planning.

Uncertainty Aware Optimal Control

Developed a robust method for reducing the sensitivity of trajectory optimization problems to parameter uncertainties with reduced computational load compared to conventional desensitization methods.

Technologies Used: MATLAB, Optimization, Control Theory.

State Estimation

Developed Kalman and Madgwick filters for orientation estimation using MPU-9250 IMU and NEO-M8N GPS data.

Technologies Used: Arduino, MATLAB, Kalman Filter, Madgwick Filter.

Minimum Snap Trajectory Generator with Time Allocation

Designed a trajectory generator for quadcopters using minimum snap optimization with time allocation for smooth and efficient flight paths.

Technologies Used: Python, Optimization, Trajectory Planning.

Deep Q-Learning

Developed a reinforcement learning agent using Deep Q-Networks to achieve soft landings in a lunar environment.

Technologies Used: Python, PyTorch, Reinforcement Learning.

Robust Costate Initialization for Optimal Control Problems

Developed a new costate initialization method based on the Adjoint Control Transformation for low-thrust trajectory optimization, achieving faster convergence than traditional methods.

Technologies Used: MATLAB, Optimization, Control Theory.
