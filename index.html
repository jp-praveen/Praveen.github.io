About Me

My research focuses on robust guidance, control, and autonomy strategies for aerospace systems, including spacecraft and UAVs. Key areas of my work include: 1. Obstacle Detection and Real Time Path Planning for UAVs: To generate efficient, collision-free navigation in an obstacle filled environment. \\ 2. Phase Synchronization Controller for Multi-Rotor UAVs: To enable noise reduction for multi-rotor systems. 3. Desensitized Guidance for Spacecraft: To produce robust guidance that can handle uncertainties in the thrust produced by the propulsion system. My work combines advanced GNC theory with practical testing on multi-rotor UAVs to create safer, more reliable autonomous systems for aerospace applications.

Real-Time Collision-Free Trajectory Optimization

A Holybro X-500 V2 quadcopter platform equipped with a Pixhawk 6c is used for all data collection and evaluation of algorithms. Jetson Nano is used as the companion computer and the quadcopter is equipped with a RealSense D435 stereo camera. An Optitrack Primex 22 motion capture (MOCAP) system is used for estimating the quadcopter’s position and orientation, which is used as the data for the state estimation on the Pixhawk. YOLOv3 Tiny was used for object detection and a ROS wrapper of YOLOv3 was utilized to integrate the algorithm into the testing platform. Realsense D435 and YOLOv3 Tiny are used for Object Detection and Localizaation (ODL). For guidance, a neural-network based framework is developed for quadcopters navigating in environments with static obstacles. Using trajectories gen- erated through a successive convexification method, three neural network (NN) models: 1) NN-Trajectory, 2) NN-Parameter, and 3) NN-Segment were developed to approximate and reconstruct optimal trajectories. Additionally, a safety margin correction step was introduced to dynamically adjust position-level information of parameter points (points closest to the obstacles), ensuring collision-free trajectories with negligible loss of accuracy.

Technologies Used: Python, ROS, Gazebo, YOLO, ZED Camera.
